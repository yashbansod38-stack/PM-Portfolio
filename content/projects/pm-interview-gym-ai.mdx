# PM Interview Gym

### An AI-powered platform that simulates real PM interviews by evaluating structured thinking, communication, and product sense.

---

# 👤 Target Users:
- Final-year students
- Entry-level PM / APM aspirants (0–2 years)
- Beginners preparing for product management interviews

---

# 💥 Problem:

- No structured feedback on answers
- No evaluation of speaking/communication skills
- No real interview simulation (no follow-up questions)
- Most good platforms are paid
- Practicing felt directionless ("I solved 50 questions but didn’t know if I improved")

---

# 💡 Key Insight:

PM interviews do not evaluate correct answers.
They evaluate:
- Structured thinking
- Problem-solving approach
- Communication clarity

No existing platform evaluates all three together.

---

# 🚀 Solution:

Built an AI-powered PM interview simulator that:
- Takes voice or text answers
- Evaluates thinking, structure, and communication
- Generates follow-up questions like real interviewers
- Provides detailed feedback + model answers

---

# ⚙️ CORE FEATURES:

### 1. Voice + Text Input
- Users answer out loud or type
- Voice is transcribed and stored

### 2. Multi-step AI Evaluation Pipeline (Key Differentiator)
- **Step 1:** Structure Analysis (frameworks, clarity, flow)
- **Step 2:** Content Scoring (8 PM dimensions)
- **Step 3:** Voice Analysis (pace, filler words, pauses, pitch)
- **Step 4:** Final Score + Feedback + Model Answer

### 3. 8 Evaluation Dimensions:
- Problem Understanding
- User Segmentation
- Structured Thinking
- Solution Quality
- Prioritization
- Metrics Thinking
- Creativity
- Communication Clarity

### 4. Follow-up Questions
- AI dynamically probes deeper based on user answer

### 5. SM-2 Spaced Repetition
- Weak answers reappear automatically
- Encourages daily practice

---

# 🧠 PRODUCT DECISIONS:

- Chose voice-first approach → PM interviews require communication skills
- Used multi-step pipeline instead of single prompt → improves evaluation accuracy
- Used RAG instead of fine-tuning → faster, cheaper, easier to update
- Used SM-2 → improves retention and habit formation

---

# ⚖️ TRADE-OFFS:

- No video interviews (reduced realism but zero-cost MVP)
- RAG over fine-tuning (less deep but faster iteration)
- Not fully conversational AI yet

---

# 📊 SUCCESS METRICS:

- Score improvement over time (e.g., 60 → 80 in 30 days)
- Daily active usage (due queue completion)
- Reduction in filler words
- Better structured answers

---

# 🔁 ITERATIONS:

- Improved prompt chaining
- Enhanced scoring calibration
- Refined feedback clarity
- Upgraded RAG knowledge base

---

# 🔮 FUTURE ROADMAP:

- Peer-to-peer mock interviews
- Video-based interviews
- Company-specific prep (Google, Amazon, etc.)
- Custom evaluation preferences

---

# 🧠 LEARNINGS:

- Evaluation systems need structure, not just intelligence
- Breaking problems into steps improves AI accuracy
- Communication is as important as thinking in PM roles
- Constraints (zero budget) drive better product decisions
