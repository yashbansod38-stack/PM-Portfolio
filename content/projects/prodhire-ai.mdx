# Problem

Searching for specialized Product Management roles across generalized job boards yields noisy, low-relevance results. APMs and Senior PMs spend hours filtering through non-product jobs masquerading under PM titles.

---

# Solution

ProdHire AI is a targeted aggregation platform that specifically scrapes, identifies, and curates genuine Product Management roles. It categorizes them by level (APM, PM, Staff) and domain (Growth, Core, AI, Platform).

---

# Core Features

- **Role-level classification** — APM, PM, Senior PM, Staff PM
- **Domain filtering** — Growth, Core, AI, Platform
- **Signal-to-noise reduction** — removes mislabeled, operations, and "PM" sales roles
- **Daily refresh** — keeps listings up to date
- **One-click apply links** — directs straight to the source

---

# Why It Works

The core insight: **specificity is the product**. Generic job boards optimize for volume. ProdHire AI optimizes for relevance — the only metric that matters to a job seeker.

---

# Impact

Creates a centralized, high-signal PM job discovery ecosystem, saving job-seekers hours of manual filtering and directly connecting them with relevant opportunities.

---

# Future Scope

- Personalized role-match scoring
- Company culture signals (Glassdoor + LinkedIn)
- Compensation benchmarking per role level
- Saved searches with email alerts
