This week we heavily focused on reducing the friction of typing long responses in the AI Interview Gym. Real interviews are spoken, not typed. 

**Progress:**
- Migrated to OpenAI's Whisper API to transcribe audio on the fly.
- Updated the React recorder component to stream audio chunks instead of waiting for full completion.
- Decreased the latency of the feedback loop from 12s to roughly 4s.
